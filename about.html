About

My name is Victor Atolagbe.

I see design as the creative process of realising robust polymorphic solutions to multifaceted problems. I think experience design is best considered from a holistic point of view of creating solutions that seamlessly adapt across touchpoints of service delivery (physical, digital and otherwise).

Design Identity

I enjoy talking with people, learning and understanding their pain-points and frustrations to eventually finding a solution. It's almost as fulfilling as what a doctor feels after making a patient's life a little bit less difficult.

My Process usually always starts with, and maybe most importantly understanding the problem. My research background in the Biomedical sciences helps with the fundamentals of the scientific method of creating and testing hypotheses to understand the nature of ever-evolving problems. After that, I discuss with stakeholders to share ideas and understand different perspectives to finding the best solutions to business, social and design problems.

When not 'designing' you can find me designing: crafting chord progressions and melodies on the guitar, or crafting words to my 'great' fantasy novel, lol. I see beauty not only in how things look but more importantly, in how efficiently they fulfil their purpose.

As an 'entry-level' designer I have lots to learn, but also confident in the skills I've gained so far on this journey. I'm open to opportunities to showcase what I can do, contributing to a team of talented individuals towards a noble goal, because I ultimately believe 'two is better than one'.
I'm always up for a chat, say hi on twitter.

Let's make something together.
