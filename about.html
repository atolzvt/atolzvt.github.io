About

My name is Victor Atolagbe.

I see design as a creative process of realising robust solutions to multifaceted problems. I think experience design is best considered from a holistic point of view of creating solutions that seamlessly adapt across touchpoints of service delivery (physical, digital and otherwise).

Design Identity

I enjoy synthesizing insights from customer/user pain-points and crafting solutions that help make their lives a little easier. It's almost as fulfilling as when a doctor helps make their patient's life a bit less difficult.

My Process usually always starts with, and maybe most importantly understanding the problem. My research background in the Biomedical sciences provides me the fundamentals of the scientific method of creating and testing hypotheses to understand the nature of ever-evolving challenges. I discuss with stakeholders to share ideas and understand different perspectives to finding the best solutions to business, social and design problems.
I have a good wield of HTML + CSS, and a healthy understanding of JavaScript and Git + GitHub. Most valuable, perhaps, is my ability to learn quickly and apply knowledge & skills to problem-solving.

When not 'designing' you can find me designing: crafting chord progressions and melodies on the guitar, or crafting sentences to my 'great' fantasy novel, lol. I see beauty not only in how things look, but more importantly, in how efficiently they fulfil their purpose.

As an 'ever-learning' designer, I indeed have lots to learn, but also confident in the skills I've gained so far on this journey. I'm open to opportunities to showcase what I can do, contributing to a team of talented individuals towards a noble goal, as a 'wise-man' once told me: "two is better than one".
I'm always up for a chat, say hi on twitter.

Let's make something together.
